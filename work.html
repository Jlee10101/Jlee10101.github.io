About

I'm Jake Lee — an industrial and CMF designer working between product, retail, and brand. My practice starts with the fold: a single sheet of paper, a clear brief, and the question, "What would make this one better?"

I've worked across luxury accessories, professional diagnostics, educational tools, and consumer goods. The common thread is the in-between — the moment when an object stops being a thing and starts being a promise.
